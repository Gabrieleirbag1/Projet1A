
Objective:

Force the opposing player to break the ‘A1’ square at the top left of the board.



Sequence:

The board is a grid of squares.
The players take it in turns to play.
On each turn, a player chooses a square, and all the squares to the right of and below it are destroyed.
The maximum number of squares destroyed is 5.
The player who breaks the ‘A1’ square loses the game.



The game ends:

The game ends when square ‘A1’ is broken. The player who destroys it loses.
